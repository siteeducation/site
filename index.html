About Us

Singrauli Institute of Technical Education (SITE), established in August 2006, is a professional institution promoted by the Society for Women Awareness and Rural Economy Generation (registered under the M.P. Society Registration Act, 1973). It aims to prepare students for creative and constructive roles in modern society by providing high-quality education, life skills, and contemporary knowledge.

Academic Excellence

🎓 Undergraduate Programs

Comprehensive bachelor's degrees across diverse fields including Engineering, Business, Arts, Sciences, and Technology. Build your foundation for success.

🔬 Graduate Studies

Advanced master's and doctoral programs designed for research excellence and professional development in your chosen specialization.

💼 Professional Development

Executive education and certificate programs for working professionals seeking to advance their careers and skills.

🌐 Online Learning

Flexible digital education options that bring SITE C quality education to students worldwide.

Innovation & Research
